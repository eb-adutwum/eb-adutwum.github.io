Introduction

Hello, I am Elijah 👋🏾

I have a special appreciation for mathematics and computing. They give me the tools to reason clearly about difficult problems, especially in this era. Over time, that curiosity has matured into a research focus on intelligent systems. At the moment, I am especially interested in how multi-agent systems can be applied to healthcare, business, and forensics. I am also fascinated by robotics for rescue contexts, where intervention is risky and human safety is at stake.

Outside technical work, I am a person of faith. My faith gives direction and purpose to my work, and it shapes the kind of scientist I want to become. I also value creative expression through music; I play both drums and piano, often in church.

Recently, I have been thinking deeply about the challenge of missing persons in Ghana. After seeing how many people are reported missing, I became convinced that more intentional systems could help return more people home safely. I believe there is real room to design tools that improve prevention, tracking, and response.

Here you will find selected projects, publications, and technical notes.
