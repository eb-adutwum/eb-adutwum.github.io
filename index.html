Hello, I’m Elijah👋🏾


I have a special appreciation for mathematics and computing. They give me the tools to think clearly and carefully about difficult problems especially in this era. Over time, my curiosity has shaped into an interest in intelligent systems. Right now, I’m especially focused on how multi-agent systems can be applied to areas like health, business, and forensics. On the side, I'm also fascinated by robotics, particularly for rescue in situations like accidents or other unpredictable events where intervention is risky and human safety is at stake.


Outside of geekiness, I am a person of faith. My faith gives me direction and purpose in my work, and it shapes how I think about the kind of scientist I want to be. I also enjoy creative expression through music as I play the drums and the piano, often in church.


Recently🤔, I’ve been thinking deeply about the challenge of missing persons in Ghana after I saw the number of people who are missing on TV. I know if we are a little more intentional about this problem, we might get a lot of people back home safely. It troubles me how and I believe there is room to design tools that could make a difference, whether in prevention, tracking, or response.
